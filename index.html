Our App.

account_balance

Instant Balance & Card Details.

With Bankify, you can instantly access your account balance and card details as soon as you log in. The app’s user-friendly interface allows you to view your available funds without delay. Additionally, you can check essential card details, such as the expiration date, ensuring you're always up-to-date and prepared for any financial situation.

receipt_long

Comprehensive Transaction Overview.

Bankify provides you with a complete overview of your finances. You can easily track the total amount of your transactions, whether they are expenses or income, all in one place. This allows you to manage your budget more efficiently and have full control over your account movements. Each transaction is clearly highlighted, giving you a detailed understanding of your financial flow.

attach_money

Instant Credit at Your Fingertips.

Obtaining credit has never been easier. With Bankify, you can quickly apply for a personalized loan by simply entering the desired amount and repayment period. The process is transparent and fast, so you can receive approval within minutes. No bureaucracy, just quick solutions that put your finances at your fingertips when you need them.

swap_horiz

Seamless Quick Transfers

Transfers become incredibly simple and fast with Bankify. You can instantly send money to any user by using only their username. No need for long IBANs or complicated details. Just a few taps, and the funds are sent, allowing you to manage payments efficiently and without hassle.
